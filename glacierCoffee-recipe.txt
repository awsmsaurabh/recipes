Ingredients:
 - 1 1/2 teaspoon sugar
 - 1 1/2 teaspoon coffee
 - 1 cup milk

Directions:
 1. Mix and stir the coffee and sugar with spoon for 5 min and prepare the paste in coffee mug.
 
 2. Boil the milk and add it to paste in coffee mug.
 
 3. Keep it in frezer until it become solid.

 4. Take out the mug from frezer and enzoy the melting drop of glacier coffee.
